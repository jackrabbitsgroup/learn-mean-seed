Programmers have a saying: "In order to understand recursion, you must first understand recursion."

Recursion is a powerful algorithmic concept, useful in just about any programming language. It is also one of the most difficult topics for beginning coders (and sometimes even for experienced coders as well!).

A recursive function is a function that calls itself. That's it. You now completely understand what recursion is. Unfortunately, knowing what it is and understanding when and how to use it well are two very different things. You should not expect to become a master of recursion in a day, or even a week. It can be difficult to get your mind to think recursively; it takes some practice to do it well.

The good news: as a web developer, you won't need to worry about recursion too often; a basic understanding should be sufficient for most of your needs.
